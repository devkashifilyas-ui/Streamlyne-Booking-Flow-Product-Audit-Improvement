Design Analysis
🔴

What was wrong

  • 5-step flow for a simple booking — excessive friction
  • Machine-style labels (FIRST_NAME, EMAIL_ADDRESS) feel cold
  • 3 action buttons per step creates decision paralysis
  • Vague success: "you'll hear back in 2–3 business days"
  • Warning notice that blames the user pre-emptively
  • Tiny, low-contrast helper text — barely readable
  • Date field requires manual format (DD/MM/YYYY)
✅

What changed

  • Collapsed to 2 steps — only essential fields shown
  • Human labels with clear required indicators
  • Visual slot pickers replace freeform inputs
  • Inline progress bar sets clear expectations
  • Instant confirmation — no manual processing step
  • Summary review screen before final submit
  • Native date picker with smart min-date constraint
📊

Impact on experience

Steps to complete 5 steps → 2 steps
Form fields 11 fields → 6 fields
Confirmation time 2–3 days → Instant
Cognitive load High → Low
Error feedback None → Inline
